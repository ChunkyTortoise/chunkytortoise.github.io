Frequently Asked Questions

How do we get started?

Email me with a brief description of your project. I'll respond within 24 hours with questions and a rough scope estimate. First 30-minute discovery call is free.

What's your typical process?

Discovery (understand requirements) → Architecture (design document for your review) → Build (tests first, CI on every push) → Delivery (deployed, documented, with demo mode). Milestone billing: 40% upfront, 30% at staging, 30% at delivery.

Do you work with existing codebases?

Yes. Most work involves integrating AI capabilities into existing systems. I start with a codebase review and propose changes before writing any code.

Is there a guarantee?

Yes. If the work doesn't meet the agreed specifications in the project proposal, I'll make reasonable revisions at no additional cost. If issues can't be resolved, the final 30% payment is refunded.

Can I try before committing to a large project?

Absolutely. The Quick Wins tier ($500–$3,000 fixed price) is designed for exactly this. Start with a Prompt Library or Dashboard MVP and scale up from there.

Do you work with agencies or white-label?

Yes. Agencies get a 10% referral fee for bringing projects. White-label partnerships available for ongoing client work.
