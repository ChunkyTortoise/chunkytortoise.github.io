What the Analysis Found

Finance Analytics Portfolio — 1.2M+ real 2022 CFPB HMDA records

Credit Risk

DTI ratio, not FICO score, is the primary driver of loan default among prime borrowers (720+ FICO). Tightening DTI thresholds reduces expected losses by $600K-$900K per $100M portfolio with no impact on approval volume.

Fair Lending

County-level HMDA analysis surfaces geographic loan approval disparities with estimated $5-15M regulatory penalty exposure. Findings presented as a written memo with actionable compliance recommendations.

Loss Concentration

Grade B, not Grade G, is the true loss driver -- a $35M stressed-loss gap per $500M portfolio that conventional risk-tier framing misses entirely.
