Where SaaS Teams Lose Time and Money

Three problems that compound as you scale.

Support costs scale with users

Repetitive questions answered by expensive human agents eat margin as you grow. The same 20 questions account for 60%+ of tickets in most SaaS products.

Churn happens 30 days before the cancellation

By the time an account cancels, the decision was made weeks earlier. Usage drop-off, support ticket frequency, and login patterns all signal what's coming.

Sales teams chase low-probability leads

Without scoring, sales effort is distributed equally across leads with very different close probabilities. High-fit accounts wait while reps work cold contacts.
