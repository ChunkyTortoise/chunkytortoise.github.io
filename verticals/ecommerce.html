Where E-commerce Teams Leave Money on the Table

Three gaps that cost revenue every week they go unaddressed.

Manual competitor price checks happen weekly

Dynamic pricing requires daily or hourly data. By the time a human logs a competitor price change, the window to respond has passed.

Email sequences aren't segmented

Every subscriber gets the same message regardless of purchase history, browse behavior, or engagement. Generic sends underperform targeted sequences by a wide margin.

Marketing budget allocation is guesswork

Without attribution, you don't know which channel actually drives revenue. Last-click gives Google all the credit. Multi-touch models show the real picture.
