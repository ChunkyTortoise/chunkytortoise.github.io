Marketing Agency Eliminates 8-Hour Weekly Reporting

Situation

A digital marketing agency tracks campaigns across 5 channels (Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, email, organic) using spreadsheets. The team manually copies data from each platform into Excel every Monday to build weekly reports for 12 clients.

Complication

No visibility into which channels actually drive conversions. Attribution is gut-feel. The Monday reporting ritual takes 8 hours per week and the numbers are stale by Tuesday. Budget allocation decisions are based on last-click attribution only, missing upstream influence.

Action

Built an automated analytics platform with four attribution models (first-touch, last-touch, linear, time-decay), interactive Plotly dashboards with auto-profiling, a data cleaning pipeline for messy CSV imports, and one-click PDF report generation.

Legal Team Gets Instant Cited Answers from 500-Page Policy Manual

Situation

A compliance team maintains a 500-page policy manual across PDF, DOCX, and internal wiki formats. Employees email the legal team 30+ questions per week asking about PTO, remote work, expense policies, and compliance procedures.

Complication

Average response time is 2 business days. Answers vary depending on who replies. Ctrl+F across 15 documents misses relevant sections. No way to verify which version of a policy was cited.

Action

Built a RAG document Q&A system with hybrid retrieval (BM25 keyword search + dense vector similarity + Reciprocal Rank Fusion), source citations with page numbers, a prompt engineering lab for A/B testing answer quality, and per-query cost tracking.

BM25 Vector Search Streamlit PyPDF2

Real Estate Brokerage Qualifies Leads 24/7 with 3-Bot AI System

Situation

A real estate brokerage receives 200+ inbound leads per month from Zillow, Realtor.com, and direct inquiries. Agents manually call each lead to determine if they are a buyer, seller, or just browsing. 60% of leads go cold before first contact.

Complication

No system to distinguish a $2M pre-approved buyer from a casual browser. Agents waste hours on unqualified leads. CRM data is inconsistent. No way to route leads to the right specialist (buyer agent vs. listing agent) without a human gatekeeper.

Action

Built a 3-bot AI system: Lead Bot qualifies with a structured Q0–Q4 question flow, then hands off to Buyer Bot or Seller Bot based on a 0.7 confidence threshold. Temperature tagging (Hot/Warm/Cold) triggers priority workflows. BI dashboard tracks bot performance, commission pipeline, and market pulse.
